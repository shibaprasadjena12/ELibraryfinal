About our Library

A library is the best possible imitation, by human beings, of a divine mind, where the whole universe is viewed and understood at the same time. - Umberto Eco:: Scott Granneman
Overview :
The Central Library was initially setup in 2004 in the west block of the Institute. It moved to the main/existing building of the Institute in February 2017 and inaugurated by Hon’ble Chairman Dr. Satya Prakash Panda in presence of Mr.B.N.Panda Vice-Chairman, Dr.C.D.Panda Secretary, Dr.M.K.Roul Principal, Dr.P.K.Rautray DEAN (Administration), Dr.S.C.Mohapatra Vice-Principal and other esteemed persons of the college. The Library is spread over three floors with the total covered area of 2480 sq. mts. (approx.) and is centrally air-conditioned. The GITA Library System comprises of a Central Library and 08 departmental libraries that collectively support the teaching, research and learning in the Institute. The Central Library houses a total collection of over 35 thousands books, 98 National and International journals, 24 magazines, 3597 video cassettes and 17 Newspapers in the fields of science, engineering, humanities, literature and management. GITA Library also provides lots of e-resources like IEEE, Science Direct, Sage, Springer and Emerald etc. All in-house operations in the library are fully computerized using the LibSys software package that also provides web-based access to the online catalogue of the Library. The whole premise of Central Library is under CCTV Surveillance. Central library is supported with 24X7 high speed wifi for the library users. The GITA Library commits to;
• Envision the research, teaching, and learning needs of its users and provide better support services;
• Provide access to internal and external information resources and promote their innovation usage;
• Ensure the preservation, long-lasting availability and enhancement of library resources;
• Create hospitable physical and virtual environments for study, teaching, and research;
• Collaborate with faculty members of the college to enrich & enhance the research and learning community;
• Develop, encourage, and sustain expertise, skill, commitment and an innovative spirit in its staff.
Library Automation:
The GITA Central Library was fully automated and operated in 2010 using the integrated library management software (Libsys). It provides several modules like Acquisition, Cataloguing, Circulation, Serials and WebOPAC. GITA library is providing the smart identity card to the students and faculty members to get the library facilities. Library users check their transaction details using WebOPAC anywhere and they put their book reservation using Online Public Access Catalogue.

Library Services and Facilities
1. OPAC (Online Public Access Catalogue).
The GITA Central Library’s Online Public Access Catalogue (OPAC) can be accessed on Intranet and Internet to search all the bibliographic records available in the Central Library database through a web-based search interface or with window client of the LibSys. The OPAC can be searched by authors, titles, subjects, keywords, classified numbers and publisher names. The OPAC also provides information about new arrivals of journals/books, etc. in the Library and Library users can put the reservation for the books using Online Public Access Catalogue.
2. Reader's Assistance.
The Library professionals are always ready to provide assistance to its users ranging from location of a book to finding specific information required by a user. A suggestion book is maintained with librarian, where the users of the library can suggest measures for improvements in its facilities and services.
